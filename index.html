— FAQs

Biodiversity Net Gain FAQs

Common questions about BNG reports, planning requirements and getting started.

What is a Biodiversity Net Gain report?

A BNG report explains the existing biodiversity value of a site, the likely impact of development and how the project can deliver the required biodiversity improvement.

Do I need a BNG report for planning permission?

Many planning applications now need to show how Biodiversity Net Gain will be achieved. The exact requirement depends on the project type, site and local planning authority.

How quickly can you prepare a report?

Turnaround depends on the site and information available, but we aim to provide clear timescales at the quote stage and keep your project moving efficiently.

What information do you need from me?

Helpful information includes the site address, red line boundary, planning drawings, existing surveys, photographs and any deadlines set by your planning consultant or local authority.

Do you cover projects across the UK?

Yes, BNG Reporter supports homeowners, landowners, developers and consultants with Biodiversity Net Gain reporting and planning support across the UK.
